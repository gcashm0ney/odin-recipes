Ingredients

  • Dough
    • 2 1/3 cups, Self-rising flour
    • 1 TBSP, Sugar
    • 1/2 cups, Butter (melted)
    • 1 1/3 cups, Buttermilk
  • Additional Ingredients, Set Aside
    • 1 cup, Self-rising flour, set aside
    • 4 TBSP, Butter (melted)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Put 1 cup of self-rising flour on a cookie sheet
  3. Mix remaining ingredients in a bowl to make dough
  4. Portion the dough into 7 or 8 roughly equal parts, making each into a ball then rolling on the floured cookie sheet before setting in a baking dish
  5. Cook for 25-30 minutes until golden brown
  6. Brush melted butter on top and cook for an additional 3-5 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
