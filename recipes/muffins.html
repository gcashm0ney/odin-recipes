Banana Nut Crunch Muffins

Ingredients

Muffins
  • 1 1/2 cups, flour
  • 1/2 tsp, cinnamon
  • 1 tsp, baking powder
  • 1 tsp, baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp, salt
  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 2/3 cup, sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp, vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup, butter (melted)
Crunch Topping
  • 1/3 cup, brown sugar (packed)
  • 2 TBSP, flour
  • 2 TBSP, butter
  • 2 TBSP, oats
  • 1/3 cup, pecans (chopped)

Instructions

Using the muffin ingredients
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line a muffin pan with paper liners.
  2. Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together the bananas, sugar, egg, vanilla, and butter
  4. Add the flour mixture to the banana mixture until just combined
  5. Divide batter evenly into the 12 muffin cups
Using the crunch topping ingredients
  1. In a small bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, and butter until mixed
  2. Stir in oats and pecans
  3. Divide mixture evenly over batter in muffin cups
Baking
  1. Bake for 18-20 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean
