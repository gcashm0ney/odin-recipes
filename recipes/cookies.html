Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

Back to Homepage

Ingredients

  • Dry Ingredients
    • 1 Cup, Granulated Sugar
    • 1 Cup, Brown Sugar
    • 2 Cups, Quick Oatmeal
    • 1 Package, Chocolate Chips and/or 1 Cup, Nuts
    • 1 1/2 Cups, Plain Flour
    • 1 Tsp, Salt
    • 1 Tsp, Baking Soda
    • 1/2 Tsp, Cinnamon
  • Wet Ingredients
    • 1 Cup, Shortening
    • 2 Eggs
    • 1 Tsp, Vanilla Extract

Insrtuctions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl
  3. Mix wet ingredients in a small or medium mixing bowl
  4. Combine the ingredients
    • Note: For best results, mix until just combined. Overmixing may result in a more dense cookie.
    • Note: My mother tends to mix the chocolate chips in at this point. I don't know if it makes a difference but I can't seem to replicate hers perfectly. This may be the trick!
  5. Using spoons, scoops 2TBSP sized globs of dough and place on a cookie sheet. Do NOT roll into a ball.
  6. Cook for 11-12 minutes
  7. Enjoy!
